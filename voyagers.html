VOYAGERS

Age restriction:15 March 2021

Set in the future, the film chronicles the odyssey of 30 young men and women who are sent deep into space on a multi-generational mission in search of a new home. The mission descends into madness, as the crew reverts to its most primal state, not knowing if the real threat they face is what's outside the ship or who is in it

Main actors:Tye Sheridan, Lily-Rose Dopp and Chante Adams.
